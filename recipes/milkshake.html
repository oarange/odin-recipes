Easy Milkshake Recipe

Description

Reminiscent of counter top service and carhop days, there is nothing better than a perfect milkshake.

Luckily, making one at home is extremely easy. This easy milkshake recipe gives you the perfect ratio of milk to ice cream and is completely customizable!

Ingredients

'
  • 4 large scoops (about 1 1/2 c.) vanilla ice cream
  • 1/4 cups of milk
  • Whipped topping, for garnish
  • Sprinkles, for garnish
  • Maraschino cherry, for garnish

Directions

In a blender, blend together ice cream and milk.

Pour into a glass and garnish with whipped topping, sprinkles, and a cherry.
